Welcome to PAD (Proximal and Distal) clustering

HOW TO USE:

1. Select the peaks from the database by clicking the green button.
2. Select the genes annotation to compare the peaks against to.
3. Choose your file to upload and compare against the database.
4. Select the cut-off value which will be the threshold to separate 'proximal' and 'distal'.
5. Click Submit.
